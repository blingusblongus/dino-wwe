# WDAA Power Rankings — Week 1

Our weekly rankings combine performance, momentum, booking, and the
deeply subjective question of *who we would not want to fight in a
parking lot*. Here's where everyone lands after Episode 1.

---

## 1. Dilo DeVille *(unranked → #1)*

A win, a no-show run-in that accomplished more than a run-in would
have, a second briefcase, and a limousine exit. He did not even work
the main event and he is the most important dinosaur in the company.
If you wrote a screenplay with this plot your editor would cut it for
being unrealistic.

> *"Which one, Emperor?"* — what he said.
> *Everything.* — what he meant.

**Trajectory:** ↑↑↑

---

## 2. Anky Bronson *(#4 → #2)*

The People's Tank walked into a title match with Conference Room
confidence and walked out with Arena Presence confidence. He didn't
win the belt. He did something harder: he made every dinosaur watching
him fight believe that he *will*. The crowd hasn't chanted a name
like that in two years. We are *building* something here, and it is
being built around a dinosaur who has publicly spoken exactly seven
words all season.

**Trajectory:** ↑↑

---

## 3. Rex Kingston *(#1 → #3)*

He retains the championship and drops two spots in the power
rankings. Welcome to the part of your reign where the math stops
mattering. Rex could not finish Anky. He could not finish him in four
rounds, seven rounds, or any number of rounds. The Extinction Event
was not deployed. The Dynastic Chomp was *eaten*. His father's
insurance policy collapsed while he was mid-match, and by the
closing bell he was fighting a guy who had figured out he was
fighting a guy. There is a very specific word for this. The word is
**mortal**.

**Trajectory:** ↓↓

---

## 4. Velo Machado *(#2 → #4)*

Lost the opener. To a part-time heel who swallowed a glass vial.
Publicly. On a Thursday. The mouthpiece-in-half moment was all the
news coverage he got, and it was news coverage *of Dilo*. This man
had top-of-the-card booking lined up for Season 1 and he is now, by
any objective measure, the coldest dinosaur on the roster.

We want to believe in Velo. We also watched him lose a match he
should not have lost, to a dinosaur he has publicly mocked for six
months. The next time we see him on camera he will be doing
something reckless. You can mark it.

**Trajectory:** ↓

---

## Also Receiving Votes

- **The Emperor** *(non-competitor)*: The shadow power-ranking.
  Two weeks ago we would have had him at #1 with a bullet. Today he
  is nowhere, because *he is not in possession of his own leverage
  anymore*.
- **Dr. Mendoza** *(non-competitor)*: Quietly the MVP. Caught a
  thrown mouthpiece in mid-air. Bit it in half for his client. The
  man is a professional.
- **The briefcase** *(inanimate)*: Better character arc than most of
  the roster.
