# WDAA Episode 1 — "The Kingston Dynasty"

**Air date:** Season 1, Week 1
**Venue:** The Tar Pit Arena, Los Ángeles-Cretáceos
**Card:** 1 undercard match, 1 main event, WDAA Apex Championship on the line.

---

## Cold Open — Backstage, 8:02 PM

ANKY BRONSON sits on a metal folding chair taping his wrists. The camera
is low, at armor-level. He doesn't look up when REX KINGSTON walks past,
flanked by THE EMPEROR and two attendants carrying mirrors.

Rex stops. Turns. Walks back. Squats — *squats* — so he's eye-to-eye with
Anky, which takes a while.

> **Rex:** You're the gauntlet guy.
> **Anky:** *(taping)*
> **Rex:** The pension-check guy.
> **Anky:** *(taping)*
> **Rex:** You know the last dino who beat a Kingston for this belt was
> my father. You know who he beat? *My grandfather*. That's the guest
> list, pal. That's the whole book.

Rex unslings the Apex Championship and holds it in front of Anky's snout.
Close enough to smell. Anky's eyes flick up for the first time. His mouth
opens — just slightly — and his jaw twitches toward the belt.

The Emperor's tail flicks. Rex yanks the belt back.

> **Emperor:** You'll touch this belt Friday. Not before.

They leave. Anky finishes taping his wrists. Doesn't react. Doesn't need to.

> **🔧 Vignette effect:** Anky Motivation **+1** (8→9, then **+1 from episode
> baseline** to 10 at bell)

## Mid-show Backstage — 9:14 PM

DR. MENDOZA and DILO DEVILLE in a corridor. Mendoza's briefcase is open.
He hands Dilo a small glass vial. Dilo holds it up to the fluorescent
light. It glows green. He tips his head back, drops it into his throat,
swallows the glass with the liquid.

> **Mendoza:** Easy. You've got a match in ten minutes.
> **Dilo:** I've got a match in thirty seconds, Doctor. The rest is
> just the opening.

Dilo walks off-camera with his teeth showing.

> **🔧 Vignette effect:** Dilo Confidence **+1** (7→8)

---

## Match 1 — Velo Machado vs. Dilo DeVille

**Pre-match promo (Velo, to camera):** *"Do I look like a warm-up act? A
guy who spits for a living? Dilo — listen close — the Kingston Dynasty is
falling *tonight*, and after the dust clears, I'm taking what's mine. I
didn't leave the Pack for* this *card position. Tick-tock, briefcase boy."*

**The match:** Both dinos are brain-heavy — Velo 9, Dilo 8 — and the
opening three rounds play like a chess match with teeth. Velo reverses
Dilo's opening snap. Dilo reverses the reversal. They circle.

Mid-round 3, Dilo starts reading Velo's combo preferences — every feint
Velo commits to, Dilo is already rotating out of. The commentators think
it's the vial; the hardcore fans think it's just Dilo being Dilo.

Rounds 5 through 7, Velo can't get a rhythm. Every attempted Pack Attack
combo collapses into a counter. The Mendoza Line gets teased twice —
Mendoza mounting the apron, Dilo not needing him. *He doesn't need him.*
That's the point of the segment.

> **Result:** **Dilo DeVille def. Velo Machado by decision** (87.6 – 83.3).
> No finisher. No pin. Just seven rounds of Velo losing an argument.

**Post-match:** Dilo refuses the handshake. Velo throws his mouthpiece
at Dilo's back. Mendoza catches it in mid-air. Hands it to Dilo. Dilo
bites it in half. Crowd: half boos, half that weird WDAA-specific cheer
they do when they know they *shouldn't* cheer.

## Pre-Main Event Backstage — 9:47 PM

THE EMPEROR knocks on Dilo's locker room door. Dilo opens it wearing a
robe. The Emperor steps inside and closes the door. We stay outside with
the camera.

We hear: a briefcase opening. A whistle (Dilo's). The briefcase closing.
Footsteps. The Emperor walks out, nods once to the camera, and moves on.

> **Emperor:** *(to camera)* I've always been a patron of the arts.

*We do not cut to the briefcase. Remember that.*

---

## Main Event — Rex Kingston (c) vs. Anky Bronson
### WDAA Apex Championship

**Pre-match, Rex on the mic:** *"Four hundred thirteen days. That's longer
than you've had a job, pal. Longer than your father held *his* job. Let me
tell you what's about to happen. I hit you once. You go down. I hit you
twice. You stay down. I hit you three times because I'm a Kingston and
we* finish *things. You walk out of here a chapter in someone else's book.
Now ring the bell."*

**Anky, on the mic:** *(silent, taps the championship belt on Rex's
shoulder, then his own chest)*

**The match:** Anky eats the opening Dynastic Chomp — barely flinches.
The crowd *gasps*, because **nobody eats Rex's opener and stays up**. Rex
tries it again in round 2. Same result. Anky's armor holds. Anky doesn't
throw much — he's a wall, not a brawler — but the wall doesn't move.

Rex is visibly rattled by round 3. His father is screaming instructions
from the apron. Rex isn't listening.

**Round 4 — the run-in spot that wasn't:**

At the top of the ramp, DILO DEVILLE appears. He's holding a briefcase.
He walks two steps down the ramp. Stops. The Emperor spots him from the
apron and gestures: *now, now, now*.

Dilo doesn't move.

He stares at Rex. He stares at the Emperor. He *smiles*. Then he turns
around, walks back through the curtain, and disappears.

The Emperor's roar is audible over the crowd. Rex turns around, confused.
Anky doesn't know what just happened — he's still a wall.

**Rounds 5–7:** Rex can't put Anky away. He's never needed round 5 in
two years. Anky's offense is modest but it *lands*. The Wrecking Ball
gets teased once in round 6 — Rex slips it. Rex goes for Extinction Event
twice and can't hit the lift.

**Result:** **Rex Kingston retains by decision** (90.2 – 84.9).

Not a finish. Not a pin. The judges give it to the champion. The crowd
gives it to the challenger. Anky stands in the center of the ring. Raises
his own arm. The crowd does the rest.

---

## Closing Segment — Parking Lot, 10:58 PM

THE EMPEROR storms through the loading dock. Dilo is leaning against a
limousine, smoking.

> **Emperor:** Where. Is. My. Briefcase.
> **Dilo:** Which one, Emperor?

Dilo steps aside. Both briefcases are on the limo's roof. Identical
leather, identical brass clasps. He taps the one on the left.

> **Dilo:** This one's the payment.
> *(taps the right)*
> **Dilo:** This one's the evidence.

Dilo picks up both. Gets in the limo. Mendoza is already at the wheel.

> **Dilo:** *(through the window, to the Emperor)* Tell your son I liked
> the main event. Seven rounds is a long time for a Kingston to last.

The limo drives off. The Emperor stands alone in the parking lot.

**Cut to black.**

## Story threads live going into Episode 2

1. **The briefcase(s).** Dilo has the Emperor's payment AND some form of
   "evidence." What's in the evidence briefcase? The broadcast has not
   told us. It will.
2. **Rex's legitimacy.** First dino to last seven rounds with him in two
   years. The crowd knows. Anky knows. Rex knows.
3. **Velo's rampage.** He opened the show demanding a title shot, lost to
   a cheat, and watched Anky steal all the headlines. He is a problem
   now. He will be a problem *publicly* next week.
4. **The Emperor's move.** His attempt to rig the title defense failed
   and left him exposed. He needs a new plan. He does not have one yet.
5. **Dilo's stable.** He's been teasing "the other two" for weeks. If
   the evidence briefcase is leverage, and the payment is capital,
   Episode 2 is when he buys a roster.
