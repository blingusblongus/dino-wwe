# WDAA Season 1 — Active Roster

The **World Dinosaur Athletic Association**. Tone: Attitude Era. Four-dino
POC roster. Stats 1–10.

---

## Rex Kingston — "The Apex"
*Tyrannosaurus rex · Heel · **Reigning WDAA Apex Champion** (413 days)*

Son of legendary retired champion **King Rex Sr.** ("The Emperor"), who now
manages him from ringside. Treats the title like a family heirloom. Doesn't
train. Doesn't have to — he's a Kingston. Speaks in third person when he's
angry. Speaks in fourth person when he's *really* angry.

- **Signature:** Dynastic Chomp *(fall-away biting suplex)*
- **Finisher:** Extinction Event *(tail-assisted powerbomb)*
- **Stats:** POW 9 · DUR 9 · DEF 6 · BRA 4 · XF 6 · CONF 10 · MOT 6

## Velo Machado — "The Final Cut"
*Velociraptor · Tweener · Top contender-in-waiting*

Broke from his pack two years ago after a public falling-out with his
brothers. Fastest hands in the WDAA. Will tell you he invented the sport.
Cuts a promo like he's selling you the building. Fans cheer because he's
cool, not because he's kind.

- **Signature:** The Pack Attack *(blinding six-strike combo)*
- **Finisher:** The Final Cut *(sickle-claw DDT off the top rope)*
- **Stats:** POW 6 · DUR 5 · DEF 7 · BRA 9 · XF 8 · CONF 8 · MOT 8

## Anky Bronson — "The People's Tank"
*Ankylosaurus · Face · #1 Contender (title shot earned in 6-dino gauntlet)*

Blue-collar. Fought his way up from the Cretaceous indies. Wears his armor
taped together. Never says much. Hates the Kingston name on principle —
his father worked security at Emperor's matches for thirty years and got
stiffed on his pension. Carries that bill into every fight.

- **Signature:** Blue-Collar Barrage *(running body check into short-arm chops)*
- **Finisher:** The Wrecking Ball *(full-rotation tail lariat)*
- **Stats:** POW 7 · DUR 10 · DEF 9 · BRA 5 · XF 4 · CONF 6 · MOT 9

## Dilo DeVille — "The Venom"
*Dilophosaurus · Heel · Chaos agent, unranked by choice*

Flanked by his manager, **Dr. Mendoza**, who carries a leather briefcase
that nobody has ever looked inside. Uses toxic spit that is technically
illegal but functionally un-refereeable. Has dropped hints on three
consecutive broadcasts about "the other two." Nobody knows what he means.
Yet.

- **Signature:** The Mendoza Line *(manager distraction into blinding spit)*
- **Finisher:** The Venom Kiss *(sleeper with venom seep — knockout)*
- **Stats:** POW 5 · DUR 6 · DEF 7 · BRA 8 · XF 9 · CONF 7 · MOT 7

## Current Story State (pre-Episode 1)

- Rex has held the Apex title for 413 days. His last three defenses ended
  in round 2 or earlier. He has not gone past round 4 in two years.
- Anky won a six-dino gauntlet three weeks ago to earn this shot. The
  Emperor went on record calling the gauntlet "a rigged charity drive."
- Velo is publicly furious that Anky jumped the line. Believes *he* should
  have been given the shot based on seniority and "objective talent."
- Dilo has accepted no matches for six weeks. He has, however, been seen
  in the parking lot three separate times having conversations with
  **The Emperor**.
- The Emperor's briefcase and Dr. Mendoza's briefcase are rumored to be
  the same briefcase. This has not been confirmed.
